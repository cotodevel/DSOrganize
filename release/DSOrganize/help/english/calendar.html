The calendar is a simple month display that allows you to schedule and view events on particular days. To move between months, use the &l; and &r; buttons. Use the cursor or the stylus to select days.

To edit an event, either press &a; or tap on the day twice with the stylus.




The day that is hilighted in red is the current day according to the DS internal clock. Days in orange have text saved for that particular day. Days underlined in purple have scheduled events in the Day Planner screen. Blue signifies weekends.
