The home screen is designed to be a simple means of navigating between different applications in DSOrganize. To select any application, use the d-pad and the &a; button or your stylus. To return to the home screen from any application, press the &start; button.






Pressing the &start; button on the home screen will bring up the credits window. Press the &start; button again to return to the home screen.

To change the icon order, head to the Cofiguration page. To add your own icons to page three, see the wiki entry on shortcuts.
