The web browser is meant to be a quick and easy way to access information on the web. Currently it supports forms, links, anchors, and a few other basic html features.

To go to a site, simply type in the URL. Shortcut keys are provided on the special keyboard tab. To visit a URL posted on IRC, simply click on it on the IRC URL list tab. Favorites are stored in your data directory, and the format is on the wiki. To activate a form element, simply click on it with your stylus. To navigate, use the stylus or press &up; or &down;. To page up or page down, press the &left; or &right; buttons. The &l; and &r; buttons are used to go forward and back in your history.
