The address book is a simple vCard 2.1/3.0 viewer and editor. It displays interesting information from each vCard in the vcard directory, alphabetized by either first or last name (configurable under the Configuration page). Simply use the &up; and &down; buttons or the stylus and the scroll bar to navigate the list. Press &x; to delete a vCard file.




DSOrganize will load phone numbers and emails from either the home or work address entry, but home has a higher priority in both instances.
