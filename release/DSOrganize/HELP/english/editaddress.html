To edit text, simply enter characters on the virtual keyboard as you would on a normal keyboard. Use the d-pad to move the cursor around. The spl button switches keyboards to special character entry mode. This is useful for foreign langauge character entry. To jump between fields, use the &l; and &r; buttons or press enter. The clear button clears all fields and the delete button removes the vCard file from your card.


Seven or ten digit phone numbers and nine digit zip codes are automatically formatted upon switching fields.
